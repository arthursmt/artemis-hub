Artemis Ecosystem Overview
Artemis is a financial project where an ecosystem of aplications are connected to multiple services creating a unique experience for the users.
Focuses on multiple users, the main goal is to create the most incredible customer experience for micro enterpreneurs.
There are two aplications in this MVP: Hunt for sales agents who offer loans on site for the customers, and Gate for analyts to evaluate Loan proposals to disburst credit into customers accounts
Those apps are connected by the same backend: Arise, where services and data bases rely to create real time experience for all the users, creating real time business and opperational KPIs.
